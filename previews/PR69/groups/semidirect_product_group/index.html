The semidirect product Lie group

The semidirect product has a few choices regarding left and right:

  • for the order of the product: LeftSemidirectProductLieGroup $\mathcal G â‹‰ \mathcal H$ vs. RightSemidirectProductLieGroup $\mathcal H â‹Š \mathcal G$
  • for the GroupAction $Î±$: AbstractLeftGroupActionType $Ïƒ$ vs. AbstractRightGroupActionType $Ï„$
  • for the GroupAction $Î±_g$ w.r.t. a fixed $g âˆˆ \mathcal G$, within a group operation $h_1â‹„h_2$: ActionActsOnLeft $Î±_g(h_1)â‹„h_2$ vs. ActionActsOnRight $h_1â‹„Î±_g(h_2)$

These choices lead to different formulae, usually even all eight cases are different. We still try to document them.
